Wordle

Instructions

The mechanics of the game are simple: you will have six attempts to guess each day's secret five-letter word. Also, please note the following facts:

  • The words are in English
  • It doesn't matter if you write the words in uppercase, lowercase or with interspersed letters
  • No numbers or punctuation marks are allowed
  • The list of all attempts, including all those considered invalid, will be reflected in a list in the lower area
